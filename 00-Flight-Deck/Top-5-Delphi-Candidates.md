These 5 candidates tell a coherent story across the 6-page Delphi Solution Summary:

- **Pages 2–3** present **GSH** and **NADH** as the primary Probius QES readouts showing the cancer/aging axis. These are the metabolites we can measure directly and immediately.
- **Page 4** zooms into **Complex I** and **NRF2** as the mechanistic drivers — the enzyme where tunneling happens and the transcription factor that controls the redox shield.
- **Pages 4–5** bring in **FAD** and cryptochrome spin coherence, completing the architecture coverage and linking to magnetoreception biology.
- **Page 6** presents the **QPI model** integrating all 5 candidates into a single composite score.
