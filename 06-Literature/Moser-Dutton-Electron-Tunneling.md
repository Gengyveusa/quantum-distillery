# 📄 Moser & Dutton — Electron Tunneling in Biological Energy Conversion

**Citation:** Moser CC, Dutton PL. *Biochim Biophys Acta* series (multiple publications).

**Key Finding:** Established that electron transfer in proteins follows quantum tunneling through the protein medium, with rates predictable from donor-acceptor distance and driving force. The Moser-Dutton ruler provides a simple exponential distance dependence for biological electron tunneling.

**Relevance:** Foundational physics for Architecture 3 — mitochondrial ETC quantum transfer. Demonstrates that the entire respiratory chain operates via quantum tunneling, not classical hopping.

**Architecture:** ETC quantum transfer (Architecture 3).
