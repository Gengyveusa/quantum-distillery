# 📄 Cao et al. 2020 — Quantum biology revisited

**Citation:** Cao J et al. *Sci Adv* 6:eaaz4888 (2020).

**Key Finding:** Comprehensive review establishing quantum effects in photosynthesis, enzyme catalysis, avian magnetoreception, and olfaction as legitimate biological phenomena. Provides critical assessment of evidence quality across domains.

**Relevance:** Validates all 4 architectures in our framework — FMO coherence, enzyme tunneling, ETC transfer, and spin coherence each have peer-reviewed evidence bases.
