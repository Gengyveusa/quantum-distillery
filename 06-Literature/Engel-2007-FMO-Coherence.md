# 📄 Engel et al. 2007 — Evidence for wavelike energy transfer through quantum coherence

**Citation:** Engel GS et al. *Nature* 446:782-786 (2007).

**Key Finding:** 2D electronic spectroscopy revealed long-lived quantum coherence in the FMO complex at 77K, demonstrating wavelike energy transfer between chromophores.

**Relevance to Thesis:** Foundational evidence that quantum coherence persists in biological systems. First direct spectroscopic proof of coherent energy transfer in a photosynthetic protein.

**Architecture:** FMO excitonic coherence (Architecture 1).

**Limitations:** Initial measurements at cryogenic temperature (77K); room-temperature coherence debated in subsequent literature. Later work by Cao et al. and others refined the picture.
