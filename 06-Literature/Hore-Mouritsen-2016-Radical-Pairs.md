# 📄 Hore & Mouritsen 2016 — The Radical-Pair Mechanism of Magnetoreception

**Citation:** Hore PJ, Mouritsen H. *Annu Rev Biophys* 45:299-344 (2016).

**Key Finding:** Established the cryptochrome radical pair mechanism as the leading hypothesis for avian magnetoreception, with a detailed spin dynamics framework describing how Earth's magnetic field modulates singlet-triplet interconversion in FAD-tryptophan radical pairs.

**Relevance:** Provides the spin coherence framework for Architecture 4 (cryptochrome radical pair). Directly connects FAD flavoprotein state to quantum biological function.

**Architecture:** Cryptochrome spin coherence (Architecture 4).
