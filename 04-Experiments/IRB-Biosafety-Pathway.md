# 📋 IRB AND BIOSAFETY PATHWAY

> Regulatory roadmap for human subjects research in the Quantum Distillery program.

## IRB Requirements

> [!warning] HUMAN SUBJECTS APPROVAL REQUIRED
> All human biospecimen collection requires IRB approval before any samples are collected. No exceptions.

| Institution | Protocol Type | Status | Target Date |
|---|---|---|---|
| UCB | Expedited Review | Planned | 2026-04 |
| UCSF | Expedited Review | Planned | 2026-04 |

## Study Classification

- **Risk level**: Minimal risk (finger-stick blood collection)
- **Review category**: Expedited review Category 2 — collection of blood samples by finger stick, heel stick, ear stick, or venipuncture
- **Determination**: No greater than minimal risk to participants

## Biosafety

- **BSL-2** required for all human blood handling
- **IBC registration** required at UCB for Lanzara lab
- **Universal precautions** per OSHA Bloodborne Pathogen (BBP) standard 29 CFR 1910.1030
- Annual BBP training for all personnel

## Consent

- **Written informed consent** required for all participants
- Include consent for biospecimen storage and future use
- **HIPAA authorization** for protected health information
- Consent documents in English (additional languages as needed per cohort demographics)
